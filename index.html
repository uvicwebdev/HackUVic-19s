Happening on March 23rd, 2019 @ UVic

What is HackUVic?

HackUVic is a hackathon hosted by the Uvic Web Design and Development Club at the University of Victoria. It's an event for developers, marketers, entrepreneurs, and designers of all skill levels to build their own project from scratch.

The event is free and food will be provided. Prizes for winners. Recommended team size is 2 - 4, we will pair you up with other students attending the event if you can't find a team.
